# Open Source Release — Engine Name: Soleri

## Context

Naming process: evaluated 40+ candidates across multiple rounds. All meaningful English words in the AI/memory space are taken (Engram, Daemon, Myco, Mneme, Rhizome — all RED). All short common words are taken on npm. All invented derivatives had conflicts (Calimo — trademark filing, Varo — Varo Bank). Winning strategy: proper nouns with a story that are ownable because they're specific, not descriptive. Soleri has zero footprint in tech — no npm package, no active tech companies, no AI products, no USPTO software trademark filings.

## Principle

The engine is named Soleri, after Paolo Soleri (1919-2013), Italian architect who coined 'arcology' (architecture + ecology) and built Arcosanti — an experimental city designed to grow organically. He studied under Frank Lloyd Wright and believed structures should be alive, adaptive, and evolving. Soleri is the invisible architect underneath Salvador, Gaudi, and all other personas.

## Example

CLI: soleri forge my-assistant | soleri update | soleri doctor | soleri list. Packages: @soleri/core, @soleri/cli, @soleri/forge. GitHub: soleri-ai/soleri. Tagline: An open-source framework for building AI assistants that learn, remember, and grow with you.

## Why

Paolo Soleri built self-sustaining living systems (arcology). The framework builds self-sustaining AI assistants that grow organically. The lineage: Salvador (Dali, surrealist vision) and Gaudi (organic architecture) are personas built on Soleri (the invisible structural genius underneath). The name is Italian, warm, 6 letters, CLI-friendly, instantly pronounceable, and tells a story.

# Open Source Release — Soleri Brand Narrative

## Context

The naming convention where personas are named after artists/architects becomes part of the brand identity. Users forge their own personas and can name them anything, but the official personas carry the artistic DNA. Soleri is not a persona — it is the foundation that all personas stand on. This mirrors Paolo Soleri's role: not the flashy artist, but the structural visionary who made living systems possible.

## Principle

Brand narrative for Soleri: Paolo Soleri believed buildings should be alive, adaptive, evolving — not static boxes. He coined 'arcology' to describe self-sustaining living architecture. Soleri (the framework) follows the same philosophy: AI assistants that are alive (they learn), adaptive (personas switch context), and evolving (brain grows with every session). The artistic lineage is: Salvador (Dali — surrealist vision, the flagship persona), Gaudi (organic architecture, another persona), all running on Soleri (the invisible living architecture underneath).

## Example

README hero: 'Soleri — An open-source framework for building AI assistants that learn, remember, and grow with you. Named after Paolo Soleri, the architect who believed structures should be alive.' Marketing angle: 'Salvador sees design. Gaudi sees architecture. Sentinel sees security. Soleri is the living foundation they all grow from.'

## Why

A strong brand narrative converts 'just another framework' into a movement. The Soleri story gives the project an identity, a philosophy, and a lineage that resonates with creators and builders. It is not a generic tech name — it carries meaning that deepens the more you learn about it.
